Understanding the Models

When it comes to software and app development in India, businesses often choose between the freelancing model and the agency model. Each has its own advantages and challenges.

Freelancing Model

  • Flexible and cost-effective
  • Direct communication with experts
  • Ideal for small projects and startups

Agency Model

  • Structured teams and processes
  • Project management and accountability
  • Best for large-scale and complex projects
