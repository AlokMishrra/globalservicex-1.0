The Story of Alok Mishra & Global Services

Alok Mishra, the founder of Global Services, started with a vision to revolutionize India's tech and freelancing landscape. With a passion for technology and a drive to empower others, Alok built Global Services into India's fastest-growing platform for software, app development, and freelancing.

From humble beginnings, Alok Mishra's journey is a testament to hard work, innovation, and a commitment to excellence. Today, Global Services connects thousands of businesses and freelancers, driving digital transformation across India.

Vision & Mission

Alok Mishra's vision for Global Services is to create a trusted ecosystem where businesses and freelancers thrive together. The mission is to deliver world-class software solutions, foster innovation, and support India's growing digital economy.
